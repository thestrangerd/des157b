Feasibility Report

Features

My AI-training law game will present various moral scenarios that players must choose between. Each decision will train the AI in different categories (i.e. empathy, nihilism, etc.). At the end, there will be a simulated "real life" scenario where users can see their AI make important decisions for a "real" case based on the way they trained it.

  • Multi-screen moral law scenarios with multiple decisions.
  • Simulated AI scenario at the end.

Technology

The multi-screen moral scenarios will be simple screen switches using visibility or opacity. Each chosen decision will contribute toward a specific personality trait category that "trains" the AI.

I plan to achieve this using an object. Each time a decision is chosen it will increase a variable value. In the final simulation, I will use conditionals to determine which decision the AI chooses, depending on which category was most favored by the player (highest value). However, I'm not sure if this is the best method to achieve this. I also do not plan on including any libraries, but may consider some if needed.
